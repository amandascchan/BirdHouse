Drones For Access

Never understating the importance of access, we've built off existing drone infrastructure to handle the unique challenges associated with inspecting bridges (or tunnels). Inspectors can access conventionally difficult to reach places, including underneath bridge decks. No heavy machinery required.

Analysis and Reporting

Measurements are the core of inspecting. With an intuitive tablet app, inspectors can take real-time measurements of cracks and spalls. To top it off, we have digitized OSIM so creating and using inspection checklists is a breeze.

Maximize Data Gain During Visual Inspections

Features

Thermal IR Imagery is used to detect subsurface cracks and delamination. Conventional inspection techniques do not provide non-destructive methods of detecting these defects. However, delamination under the bridge deck can raise crucial safety concerns for passing by traffic.

Semi-autonomous drones in combination with an intuitive interface enables precision during access.

Full access to under the bridge. Localization technology enables access to GPS denied locations, such as under the bridge deck.
